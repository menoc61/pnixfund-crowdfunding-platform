      Comment Manager

      Campaign Comment Control Hub

      As an admin responsible for comment management on the PnixFund platform, you play a crucial role in fostering a positive and engaging community environment. This comprehensive guide is designed to equip you with the tools and insights needed to effectively moderate comments posted on campaigns across the platform.

      • Comment Moderation :
        Admins can view a list of comments posted on campaigns across the platform.
        Admins have the ability to approve or delete comments based on platform guidelines.
        Enables admins to manage comments efficiently and ensure compliance with community standards.
      • Registered and Guest User Comments :
        Allows both registered users and guest users to post comments on campaigns.
        Enhances user engagement by providing a platform for users to share feedback, ask questions, and express support for campaigns.
        Admins can monitor and moderate comments from both registered and guest users to maintain a positive and respectful community environment.
      • Search Feature :
        Allows admins to search for comments by campaign or author name.
        Streamlines the process of finding and managing comments, saving admins time and effort.

      By mastering the comment management features outlined in this guide, you're equipped to foster a positive and engaging community environment on the PnixFund platform. Whether you're moderating comments, engaging with users, or analyzing comment activity, your efforts are instrumental in shaping the success of campaigns and building a supportive community of changemakers.
