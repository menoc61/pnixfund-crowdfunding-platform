      Campaign Manager

      Campaign Control Hub

      Welcome to PnixFund Crowdfunding Campaign Management!

      As a campaign manager on the PnixFund platform, you have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact by overseeing and optimizing fundraising campaigns. This comprehensive guide is designed to empower you with the tools and insights needed to effectively manage campaigns and drive success.

      • Campaign Approval Management: :
        Admins can view a list of pending campaigns awaiting approval.
        Admins have the ability to approve or reject pending campaigns based on platform guidelines and criteria.
        Enables admins to efficiently manage the queue of pending campaigns and ensure compliance with platform policies.
      • Donation Details :
        Displays the percentage of the campaign goal amount that has been raised through donations.
        Admins can access detailed information about donations received, including donor details, donation amounts
      • Campaign Gallery Showcase: :
        Admins can showcase images related to campaigns in a gallery format.
        Enhances campaign visibility and engagement by providing donors with visual insights into the campaign's impact and progress.
        Offers a user-friendly interface for admins to upload, manage, and organize campaign gallery content.
      • PDF Documentation View: :
        Admins can view PDF documentation associated with campaigns directly within the admin panel.
        Facilitates efficient review and approval processes by centralizing campaign documentation within the admin panel.
      • Featured Campaign Option: :
        Admins can designate campaigns as "featured," highlighting them on the platform's homepage or dedicated sections.
        Increases the visibility of featured campaigns, attracting more attention from potential donors and supporters.

      By mastering the campaign management features outlined in this guide, you're equipped to drive impactful results and make a difference in the world. Whether you're approving pending campaigns, tracking donation insights, or showcasing campaign content, your efforts are integral to the success of the PnixFund platform.
